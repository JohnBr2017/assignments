The Primes of Warframe!

Hydroid Prime!

August 29th, 2017

Hydroid Prime is the primed variant of the Hydroid Warframe featuring more powerful stats: increased armor, energy and shield ratings, as well as an additional Vazarin Pol and Naramon Pol polarity. It was released alongside Ballistica Prime and Nami Skyla Prime.

Oberon Prime!

May 30th, 2017

Oberon Prime is the primed variant of the Oberon Warframe featuring more powerful stats: 25 extra energy and 75 extra armor, as well as two additional Naramon Pol polarities, and a new particle effect on his Reckoning ability and his Hallowed Ground ability.

Banshee Prime!

February 28th, 2017

Banshee Prime is the Prime variant of the Banshee Warframe featuring higher armor, sprint speed, and power values, as well as an additional Naramon Pol polarity.
